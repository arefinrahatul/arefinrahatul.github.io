About Me

Dedicated to streamlining VAT compliance and financial reporting workflows

I am a dedicated and detail-oriented Corporate Finance and NBR VAT compliance specialist with over 3 years of hands-on industry experience. I hold both an MBA and BBA in Accounting from Tejgaon College, Dhaka, which provides me with a robust theoretical foundation to solve complex corporate financial problems.

Throughout my career at top-tier corporate groups like Padma Group of Converters and Hop Yick (BD) Ltd., I have focused on:

  • Statutory VAT Compliance: Maintaining NBR-compliant corporate VAT registers (including Mushak 6.1, 6.2, 6.3, 6.4, 6.6, 6.7, 6.8) and managing return submissions.
  • TDS & VDS Deductions: Preparing accurate calculations, deduction certificates, and monthly tax reconciliation reports.
  • Financial Operations & Audit: Leading inventory stock reconciliation, preparing sales analysis, and managing transactions using ERP systems like SAP and Symphony.

I thrive on bringing automation to manual accounting processes, minimizing tax risks, and ensuring corporate files are 100% compliant with National Board of Revenue regulations.
